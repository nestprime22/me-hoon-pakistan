Important Days of Pakistan

⬇️ Download Video

Pakistan Day – 23rd March

Pakistan Day commemorates the Lahore Resolution in 1940 which paved the way for the creation of Pakistan. It also marks the adoption of Pakistan’s first constitution in 1956.

⬇️ Download Video

Independence Day – 14th August

Independence Day celebrates Pakistan’s freedom from British rule in 1947. The day is marked by patriotic displays, flag hoisting ceremonies, parades, and cultural programs throughout the country to honor the sacrifices made for independence.

⬇️ Download Video

Defence Day – 6th September

Defence Day honors the sacrifices of Pakistani soldiers during the 1965 war with India, celebrating the nation’s resilience and courage.

⬇️ Download Video

Kashmir Solidarity Day – 5th February

Kashmir Solidarity Day is observed to show support for the people of Kashmir and highlight their struggle for freedom.

Youm-e-Takbeer – 28th May

Youm-e-Takbeer marks the historic day when Pakistan conducted its nuclear tests in 1998, becoming the first Muslim nuclear power. This day symbolizes national strength, defense capability, and technological advancement.

⬇️ Download Video

Quaid-e-Azam Day – 25th December

Quaid-e-Azam Day commemorates the birth anniversary of Muhammad Ali Jinnah, the founder of Pakistan, honoring his leadership and vision.
